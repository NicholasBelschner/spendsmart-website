Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

Note: This is a summary. For the complete privacy policy, see the full text version.

Introduction

SpendSmart is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Transaction Data: Amounts, dates, merchant names, categories (from manual entries or bank connections)
  • Bank Connection: Secure tokens via Plaid (read-only access)
  • Device Information: iOS version, app version (for support)

How We Store Your Information

All transaction data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not transmit your transaction data to external servers.

Data Sharing

We share banking connection requests with Plaid to establish secure access. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Your Rights

  • Access your data within the app
  • Delete individual or all transactions
  • Disconnect bank account anytime
  • Export your data
