About

SpendSmart is a minimalist financial mindfulness app that helps you see the hidden cost of your spending. Every dollar you spend is time traded. This app shows you what that money could become if invested instead.

No guilt, no hype. Just clear awareness of what you're trading for each purchase.

Features

  • Track expenses manually or connect your bank via Plaid
  • See missed investment opportunities with compound interest projections
  • Category-based insights with motivational alternatives
  • Clean, minimalist design focused on clarity
  • Privacy-first: All data stored locally on your device

Philosophy

Money = Time
Every dollar you spend is life hours traded. Choose how you spend your finite time wisely.

Own Less, Experience More
Freedom comes from owning less. Seek free activities: walking, hiking, conversations, window-shopping, home brewing, sober game nights.

Mindful Choices
